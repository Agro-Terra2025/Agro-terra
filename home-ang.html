    Agro-Terra

    Agro-Terra is a platform specialized in agriculture, designed to inform and support farmers, researchers, and other professionals in the sector. It provides resources on agricultural techniques, innovations, sustainable management, as well as market news and trends. It also serves as a space for exchanging experiences and expertise to promote the development of efficient and sustainable agricultural practices.

    Smart Irrigation: Save Water While Efficiently Nurturing Your Plants

    Discover our revolutionary irrigation program: a solution that optimizes every drop of water to meet plant needs while preventing waste. Farm smarter, conserve resources, and ensure optimal growth for your crops.

    Planting Tips

    Planting Tips is an interactive feature that helps farmers optimize their planting by leveraging geolocation, real-time weather data, and best agricultural practices to provide personalized recommendations.

    Weather

    The weather feature retrieves local climate data (temperature, feels-like temperature, humidity, wind) and displays tailored advice, using different colors to represent temperature ranges.
