    Smart Irrigation

    Smart irrigation uses sensors to measure soil moisture and weather conditions, automatically adjusting water delivery for each crop. It allows remote management through apps, providing real-time monitoring. By integrating weather forecasts, it optimizes water usage based on the actual needs of the plants. The goal is to reduce waste, conserve resources, and ensure optimal crop growth.
